# Power BI Definitions

## Early Scoping
- Like presales or first meeting

### Use Cases
- How many users? Do you want to build reports or just view?
    - Developers?
    - Viewers?
- How many source systems? 
    - What systems?
- How many reports?
    - For who?
    - What type?

### Current Stuff
- Development practices?
    - Governance?

### Data Refresh
- When is your source data ready to use?
- How often does your source data change?
- What time do you need your data by?
- How many times do you need to refresh in a day? What is the business need?
- How important is keeping data up-to-date versus report performance?

## Report Scoping
- The detailed stuff

### Who?
- Who is using the report?
- What are the responsibilities?

### Key questions
- What questions should be answered?
- What information is needed?

### Decisions / Use Cases
- What is decided based on information?
- Example use cases

### Measures
- How can you measure key questions and decision making?

### Perspectives
- Which perspectives are important?
- Is a trend needed? Compare by?
- What information is sensitive? GDPR?

### Data Sources
- Where to get the data?
- Who is the expert on the system?
