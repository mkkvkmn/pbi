# Reports
Power BI reports are built on top of datasets. Datasets get most of their data from ...

... is a database containing most of the data from systems used by our company and data is managed by ...

Because ... does not contain all the data needed, Power BI datasets are complemented with Excel / Google Sheets data.

Detailed documentation of the data available is in the Power BI Governance section 4. Environment.

## Datasets & Tables for Official Reports
Power BI report authors are granted access to official data sources.

General rule is that fact tables contain aggregated data (e.g. sales) and dimension bring the perspective (e.g. by customer).
